# Graphene‑Lab

Graphene‑Lab is an open‑source research group focused on building secure, privacy‑preserving digital infrastructures. Its work spans blockchain engineering, cryptographic protocol design, decentralized applications, and secure key‑management systems. The organization develops and maintains a broad ecosystem of technologies, including custom blockchain protocols such as the DCD protocol, blockchain explorers, decentralized exchange mechanisms, smart‑contract systems, and tools for encrypted storage and identity protection.   [Github](https://github.com/GrapheneLab/)

The laboratory’s repositories demonstrate a strong commitment to transparency and resilience, with implementations ranging from low‑level C++ blockchain components to TypeScript libraries, Solidity‑based token systems, and user‑facing applications. These projects support secure communication, verifiable computation, and privacy‑centric digital interactions, reflecting a long‑term vision of trustworthy and censorship‑resistant infrastructures.   [Github](https://github.com/orgs/GrapheneLab/repositories)

Graphene‑Lab’s work integrates applied cryptography, distributed‑ledger technologies, and secure software engineering to advance the state of open‑source cybersecurity. Its mission is to provide robust foundations for next‑generation privacy technologies and to enable individuals and organizations to operate safely in adversarial environments.   [Github](https://github.com/GrapheneLab/)
